# 🌿 Plant Disease Detector for Farmers

An AI-powered web application that helps farmers identify plant diseases using their smartphone camera.

## ✨ Features

- 📸 **Back Camera Support** - Automatically uses back camera for best photos
- 🤖 **AI-Powered Detection** - Instant disease identification
- 💊 **Treatment Recommendations** - Both chemical and organic solutions
- 🛡️ **Prevention Tips** - Learn how to prevent future outbreaks
- 📱 **Mobile Responsive** - Works perfectly on smartphones
- 🌐 **Offline Capable** - Works without internet after first load
- 🆓 **100% Free** - No registration or payment required

## 🎯 Supported Diseases

- Early Blight (Tomatoes, Potatoes)
- Powdery Mildew (Cucurbits, Grapes)
- Late Blight (Potatoes, Tomatoes)
- Leaf Spot (Beans, Beets, Carrots)
- Rust (Wheat, Barley, Beans)
- Bacterial Blight (Rice, Beans, Citrus)
- Fusarium Wilt (Tomatoes, Bananas)
- Downy Mildew (Grapes, Cucumbers)
- Anthracnose (Mangoes, Beans)
- Mosaic Virus (Tomatoes, Cucumbers)

## 📋 How to Use

1. **Open the app** on your smartphone or computer
2. **Click "Start Back Camera"** and allow camera access
3. **Take a clear photo** of the affected plant leaf
4. **Click "Capture & Identify"** or upload a photo
5. **Get instant diagnosis** with treatment recommendations

### Pro Tips for Best Results

- Use natural daylight for better image quality
- Focus on the most affected areas of the leaf
- Include both healthy and sick tissue if possible
- Hold the camera steady for sharp images
- Ensure the leaf fills most of the camera frame
